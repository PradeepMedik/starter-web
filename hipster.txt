DevOps is a collection of two words, “Development” and “Operations,”
 representing a cultural approach that emphasizes collaboration between 
 development and operations
 teams to streamline the entire software delivery lifecycle.

This DevOps tutorial is designed to help learn DevOps basics 
and advanced concepts, including Git, Ansible, Docker, Puppet, Jenkins, a range of DevOps tools, Azure DevOps, Chef, Nagios, and Kubernetes.
